History and Culture

Ireland's history is RICH and fascinating, with many many cultural traditions and artifacts growing from it.
For example, the Claddagh ring is a peice of jewelery usually passed down by women in a family. It represents love, both romantic and platonic. Below is a picture.

The heart represents love, with the two hands on either side representing friendship, and the crown on top represents loyalty. The Claddagh Ring is typically used to indicate romantic relationships, or lack thereof. Typically the Claddagh ring is worn on the ring finger, of both left and right hands. When worn on the right hand, with the point of the heart pointing up, or away from you, it signals that the person wearing the ring is single. On the same hand, when the heart is pointing towards you, it indicates you're in a romantic relationship. On the left hand, if the heart points away it signals engagement, and when the heart points towards it signifies marriage.
